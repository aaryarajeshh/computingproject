Global Average PM2.5 Concentration Trends

Analysis Period: 2010-2023

Key Insights

  • Global PM2.5 levels show a concerning upward trend from 2010 to 2023, rising from approximately 45 µg/m³ to 52 µg/m³
  • Current global average is over 10 times higher than the WHO guideline of 5 µg/m³, indicating a severe worldwide air quality crisis
  • The trend suggests acceleration in recent years (2020-2023), possibly linked to increased industrial activity and urbanization in developing nations
  • Without intervention, models predict global PM2.5 could exceed 60 µg/m³ by 2030
