Urbanization Rate vs PM2.5 Concentration

2023 Data

Previous: GDP Correlation Home Next: Health Impact

Key Insights

  • Moderate positive correlation (r = 0.45) between urbanization and PM2.5, but the relationship is complex and income-dependent
  • High-income countries maintain low PM2.5 even at 80%+ urbanization rates through green urban planning and public transit
  • Low and middle-income countries with rapid urbanization (60-75%) face PM2.5 spikes exceeding 60 µg/m³
  • Cities in developing nations grow 3x faster than their environmental infrastructure, creating pollution hotspots
