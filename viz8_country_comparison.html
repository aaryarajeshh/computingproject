Key Insights

  • China shows dramatic improvement: PM2.5 dropped from 82 µg/m³ (2013) to 52 µg/m³ (2023) through aggressive environmental policies
  • India's PM2.5 has risen steadily from 68 to 78 µg/m³, with no signs of reversal despite policy attempts
  • United States and Germany maintain consistently low levels below 10 µg/m³, demonstrating sustainable clean air is achievable
  • Bangladesh remains critically high at 80+ µg/m³ throughout the period, requiring urgent international intervention
